Our Legacy

Redefining Legal Excellence Since 1984

Founded by James A. Talbert in a small Manhattan office, Talbert Legal Partners began with a revolutionary idea: that a law firm could combine the rigor of a white-shoe practice with the agility and innovation of a modern enterprise. Four decades later, that vision has grown into one of the nation's most respected litigation and corporate advisory firms.

Today, we stand at the forefront of legal innovation, representing Fortune 500 companies, emerging growth businesses, and high-net-worth individuals in their most complex legal matters. Our team of 40+ attorneys brings together former federal prosecutors, SEC officials, and partners from the world's leading law firms.

What sets us apart is our relentless commitment to strategic thinking. We don't just react to legal challenges—we anticipate them, developing proactive strategies that protect our clients' interests and advance their objectives.
